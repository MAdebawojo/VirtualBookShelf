Virtual Book Shelf

About this Project

Description:

Virtual Bookshelf is a web application designed and developed by Adebawojo Mosopefoluwa. It serves as a digital platform for book enthusiasts to organize and manage their book collections. With Virtual Bookshelf, you can effortlessly catalog your favorite books, and keep track of their reading progress.

Key Features:

  • Add and Organize Books: Easily add books to your virtual bookshelf by providing essential details such as book title, authors, a brief description, and its reading status (To Read, Reading, or Complete).
  • Edit Book Details: Need to update the information about a book? Virtual Bookshelf allows you to edit book titles, descriptions, authors, and reading status.
  • Effortless Book Removal: When you're done with a book, you can remove it from your collection with just a click.
  • User-Freindly Imterface: The application offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it simple to navigate and manage your bookshelf
  • Personalized Reading Experience: Keep track of books you're currently reading, those you intend to read, and those you've completed.
  • Secure User Accounts: User accounts are secured with password hashing, ensuring the safety of your personal information.
